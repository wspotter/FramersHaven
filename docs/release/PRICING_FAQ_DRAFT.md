# Pricing FAQ (Draft)

This is a draft FAQ for early-access pricing conversations. Final numbers and offer terms require local review and human approval before publication. This document does not create a legal offer or obligation.

## Where will pricing be published?

Pricing should be published on the public FramersHaven website or sales page after human review. Keep dollar amounts out of the source repository so draft numbers do not become accidental public offers.

Current repo-safe assumptions:

- Community Edition is planned as free and source-available.
- Workstation Windows ZIP/folder packaging is planned as a paid offer.
- Setup/support calls may be offered separately.
- Update terms must be written plainly before any sale.

These are product-planning notes, not prices or final offers.

## Is Workstation a one-time purchase or a subscription?

The current draft assumption is a paid early-access Workstation Windows ZIP/folder workflow. Final purchase model, update window, and support terms belong on the public website or sales page after review.

## What does the setup call include?

The setup call is intended to help an operator get the Windows workstation running, review the local-first workflow, and confirm that catalog imports, document templates, and accounting CSV export are working as expected. It does not include custom development, data migration from other POS systems, or ongoing bookkeeping support.

## Can I try FramersHaven before paying?

Yes. Community Edition is free and source-available. It includes the core local workflow: manual catalog entry, quotes/orders/customers, PDF/JPG exports, and manual backups. It is scale-limited but fully usable for a small local trial.

## Does Workstation include vendor catalogs or customer records?

No. FramersHaven does not ship vendor catalogs, customer records, or accounting credentials. Catalog imports use operator-supplied local files only.

## Does Workstation connect to accounting software?

No. Workstation Edition includes local CSV export for accounting review. It does not connect to an accounting service, prove that records were accepted, or reconcile transactions automatically. There is no accounting API sync.

## What is the refund/support expectation?

Early access is sold as-is with updates through v1.0 included. Refund and support expectations should be written plainly in the final offer terms before any sale. This draft does not create a legal obligation.

## Can I upgrade from Community to Workstation later?

That is an open product question. The current draft assumes Workstation is a separate paid Windows build. Future upgrade paths, license keys, or migration support are not defined yet.

## Who is this not for?

FramersHaven is not a POS replacement, hosted service, or accounting platform. It is not intended for shops that need payment processing, native SMS delivery, or automatic accounting sync on day one.
